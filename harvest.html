WELCOME TO HARVEST MISSION SCHOOLS

We commit to the next generation

Harvest Mission Schools is a lively learning community with thousands of students from Nursery to A-Level. It's a space for curious and ambitious learners to grow mentally, creatively, and personally.

Our teachers deliver top-tier lessons that spark curiosity and keep students engaged. We focus on building not just academic skill, but strong character, leadership, and responsibility.

Started with a vision to uplift education in Kagera, Harvest Mission has grown into one of Tanzania's trusted institutions. We're dedicated to shaping students who can thrive in a fast-changing world.
